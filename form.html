Website Planning Document

Site Name: GirlFit Studio

This site is named GirlFit Studio because it focuses on empowering women in their fitness journeys with accessible workout routines, tips, and resources for home and gym use.

Optional Domain: girlfitstudio.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of GirlFit Studio is to provide a hub for women interested in health and fitness. The site will include beginner-friendly workout plans, nutritional tips, and a form to sign up for virtual coaching or newsletters. It’s designed to be motivational, supportive, and easy to use.

Scenarios

  • What’s a good beginner workout plan I can do at home?
  • How do I sign up for a virtual fitness class?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #EF5DA8 (Pink) - Used for buttons, links, and headings.

Secondary Color: #5DEFC5 (Mint) - Used for accents and backgrounds.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
  • Headings will be bold and engaging; body text will be clean and easy to read.
